SwiftBuilder Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Summary: SwiftBuilder Pro is a local-first app. Project/workspace content is stored on your Mac. No third-party ad tracking SDKs are used.

1. Data We Process

  • Project/workspace data: Screens, components, settings, and generated output metadata saved locally on your device.
  • Purchase entitlement state: Subscription access status provided by Apple StoreKit.
  • Support contact data: Information you choose to send via email (message text, attachments, optional diagnostics).

2. Data We Do Not Use for Tracking

  • No cross-app tracking for advertising.
  • No third-party behavioral profiling.
  • No sale of personal data.

3. Purchases and Billing

Payments and subscription billing are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card data. We only use entitlement information necessary to unlock Pro features.

4. Local Storage and Exported Files

Workspace/project content is stored locally. Exported Swift files are saved to folders you select.

5. Security

We apply reasonable safeguards for local app data handling. You are responsible for protecting your Mac account and any exported files.

6. Data Retention

Local project data remains until you delete it. Support communications are retained only as needed for support/legal obligations.

7. Children's Privacy

SwiftBuilder Pro is a developer tool and is not directed to children under 13.

8. Your Choices

  • Delete local app data from the app workflow or file system.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple subscription settings.

9. Contact

10. Changes to this Policy

Policy updates may occur over time. The “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
